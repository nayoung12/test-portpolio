#1 “Surprise”

FF is hosting an open call for solo exhibition proposals. The theme for our first open call is “Surprise.” We are looking forward to encountering a variety of experimental and challenging works. Artists planning new exhibitions, we eagerly anticipate your active interest and participation!

🔸Eligibility: No restrictions
🔸Field: Visual Arts
🔸Application Period: Until midnight, April 30th (Tuesday)
🔸Selection: One team - individuals or groups are both welcome (Selected team will be contacted individually in early May)
🔸Support Provided: Exhibition space support, promotion including poster design and video production, and event support tailored to the exhibition plan
🔸How to Apply: Submit via email (finalfrontier.seoul@gmail.com)
🔸Required Documents: One portfolio file named “Portfolio_ArtistName.pdf” (in a free format including up to 10 works, curriculum vitae, artist statement, contact information, and exhibition plan, file size under 10MB)

*Email Subject Format: [FF Open Call_#1]_ArtistName
*Inquiries: finalfrontier.seoul@gmail.com / Instagram DM available
*Exhibition period, installation and de-installation will be discussed during the meeting.

Consists of 8 artists based in Seoul,
FF pursues making decent artistic communication among creators from various fields, transcending national boundaries. We are absolutely open to every opportunity to step up together.
So feel free to get in touch with us!
